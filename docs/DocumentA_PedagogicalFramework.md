2. STPME Framework ‚Äî The Five Engines of Linguins
The pedagogical foundation of Linguins is built on five engines that work together to create a complete learning experience.

2.1 SKILL ENGINE
Internal measurement of real learning. (Invisible to the user)
It tracks:
    mastery of items (words, phrases, concepts)
    retention
    speed
    accuracy
    writing abilities
    pattern recognition
This engine determines what the user truly knows‚Äînot what they ‚Äúcompleted.‚Äù


2.2 TRACKING ENGINE
External visibility of progress. (Visible to the user)
Transforms internal learning data into meaningful insights:
    weekly progress
    strengths & weaknesses
    accuracy evolution
    speed improvements
    consistency patterns

Tracking = clarity.
 Skill = reality.


2.3 ADAPTIVE ENGINE
The pedagogical director of the platform.
Responsible for:
    deciding what the user should study today
    balancing review vs. new content
    adjusting difficulty dynamically
    detecting frustration and stagnation
    recommending modes based on available time
    generating ‚Äúsoft re-entry sessions‚Äù after inactivity
This engine ensures personalized and frictionless progress.


2.4 MOTIVATION ENGINE
Drives healthy, adult motivation.
Provides:
    positive reinforcement
    recognition of effort
    micro-goals
    momentum-building messages
    non-childish gamification
Its goal is to foster constancy and psychological reward, without gimmicks.


2.5 EMOTIONAL ENGINE
Human-centered guidance and emotional support.
Responds to:
    frustration
    low energy
    fear of mistakes
    lack of confidence
    motivation dips

    ‚ÄúThis is a normal challenge at your level.‚Äù
    ‚ÄúLet‚Äôs keep today light; you‚Äôre doing well.‚Äù

3. Learning Atom ‚Äì The Minimum Learning Unit
To build a flexible and scalable learning system:
‚úî Item = universal minimum unit of study
An item can represent:
    a word
    a short phrase
    a chunk
    a simple concept (article, preposition, verb pattern)

This provides:
    total modularity
    consistent behavior across all modes
    future-proofing for advanced learning features
    compatibility with STPME engines
All study modes operate on Items.

6. AI Integration Strategy
AI is used strategically, not excessively.
 The goal is support, not replacement of learning.

6.1 AI IS USED FOR:
    correcting writing (Feedback Mode)
    generating emotional support messages
    summarizing insights in natural language
    guiding micro-coaching interactions
future advanced personalization (v6.2)



6.2 AI IS NOT USED FOR:
    selecting new vocabulary items
    running SRS (this is database-driven for speed)
    correcting advanced grammar
    generating large amounts of content
    creating full lessons
AI exists to enhance clarity and human connection, not to overwhelm the user or slow the experience.

    complex multimedia modes (Voice, Social, Explorer, Challenge)

v6.1 is a focused, minimal, high-impact version.
